Katie's List

Top 3 Movies of All Time

Howl's Moving Castle

This movie has incredible animation and a captivating story. I loved this movie so much I even named my dogs after Howl and Sophie.

Garden State

This wonderful indie film dives into the difficulties of mental health, friendship, family, and love. It's a reminder of what matters most in life.

Inception

Mind-bending mystery and thrills always make a movie great. The story behind this film is wonderfully complex and makes for a fun ride.
